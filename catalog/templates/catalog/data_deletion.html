🗑️ Data Deletion Instructions

How to delete your data from BGHrana.com

For Facebook Login Users:
If you signed up using Facebook Login, follow these instructions to delete all your data from our platform.

How to Delete Your Data

Option 1: Delete via BGHrana.com
  1. Log in to your account at BGHrana.com
  2. Go to your Profile Settings
  3. Click on "Delete Account" button
  4. Confirm deletion by entering your password
  5. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted
Option 2: Contact Support

If you cannot access your account, contact our support team:

  • Email: support@bghrana.com
  • Facebook Messenger: Send Message

Include the following information in your request:

  • Your email address used for registration
  • Request: "Please delete my account and all associated data"

We will process your deletion request within 30 days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your email address and account credentials
  • Profile information (name, username)
  • All product listings you created
  • Product images you uploaded
  • Contact information (phone, Viber, Messenger links)
  • Account activity history
⚠️ Important: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you want to delete all your data before proceeding.

Deletion Timeline

  • Immediate: Your account is deactivated instantly
  • Within 24 hours: All your public data (listings, profile) is removed from the site
  • Within 30 days: All data is permanently deleted from our systems and backups
